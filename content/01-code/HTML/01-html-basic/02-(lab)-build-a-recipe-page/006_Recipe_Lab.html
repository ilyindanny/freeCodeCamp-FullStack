Beautiful Cement recipe

Tired of boring, plain cement? This ultimate guide will teach you how to mix and pour beautiful, smooth, aesthetically pleasing cement that will make your sidewalks the envy of the neighborhood. Whether you're building a patio or sculpting a brutalist masterpiece, this recipe has you covered.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of industrial-strength cement mix
  • 1 cup of sparkling quartz dust
  • Half a liter of purified moon water (or tap water if you're on a budget)
  • 3 drops of essential oil (for ambiance only — do not mix!)
  • 1 pair of safety goggles, preferably rhinestone-studded

Instructions

  1. Put on your rhinestone-studded safety goggles. Safety is fabulous.
  2. In a large, glitter-free mixing bowl, combine the cement mix and quartz dust.
  3. Slowly pour in the purified moon water while stirring gently (clockwise only — counter-clockwise ruins the vibe).
  4. Keep mixing until the texture is smoother than a luxury skincare commercial.
  5. Let the cement rest for 5 minutes while you post a story about it.
  6. Pour the mix into your chosen mold — hearts, stars, or traditional blocks, your call.
  7. Let dry for at least 24 hours, ideally under the full moon.
